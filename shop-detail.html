Zima Homes, Wangige, is a premier residential development located in the heart of Wangige, just 20 minutes from the bustling Westlands area of Nairobi. This modern community features 120 spacious two-bedroom apartments designed with contemporary living in mind. Each unit offers ample natural light, modern amenities, and access to communal spaces that foster a sense of community. With its strategic location, residents enjoy proximity to essential services, schools, and recreational facilities, making it an ideal place for families and young professionals alike.

Investing in Zima Homes presents a unique opportunity to be part of a growing market in a high-demand area. By acquiring AKREs, investors can participate in the ownership of these valuable properties, benefiting from potential rental income and long-term capital appreciation. The development's commitment to quality and sustainable living ensures that it will remain a desirable choice for tenants, providing investors with a steady return on their investment. With a starting price of just KES 50 per AKRE, Zima Homes is a promising investment opportunity that combines affordability with high growth potential.
