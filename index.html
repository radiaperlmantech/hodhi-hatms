    Frequently Asked Question

    What is HATMS?

    HATMS (Hodhi Asset Token Management System) is an innovative platform that allows you to invest in real estate through digital tokens called AKREs. These tokens represent ownership in income-generating properties, providing you with access to property appreciation, rental income, and interest from government-backed bonds.

    What is an AKRE?

    An AKRE (Affordable Key to Real Estate) is a digital token that represents a fractional ownership of real estate. AKRE holders benefit from the propertyâ€™s rental income and potential value appreciation without the complexities of direct ownership. Each AKRE can be bought, sold, or traded on the Hodhi platform.

    How does tokenization work on HATMS?

    Tokenization on HATMS involves converting the ownership of physical real estate into digital tokens. Each property is divided into a finite number of AKREs, which can be purchased by investors. These tokens allow investors to own a share of the propertyâ€™s financial benefits, such as rental income and capital appreciation, with minimal investment.

    How much does an AKRE cost?

    Each AKRE is priced as low as KES 50, making it affordable for investors of all levels. The price may vary depending on the property, but the low entry point ensures that real estate investment is accessible to a wider audience.

    Can I sell or trade my AKREs?

    Yes, AKREs are liquid assets. You can buy, sell, or trade your AKREs at any time on the Hodhi platform. This provides you with flexibility and control over your investment, allowing you to respond to market conditions or financial needs.
